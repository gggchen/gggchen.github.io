Debug Ocenaudio

Oct 24, 2017

Oceanaudio is a good and beautiful software to edit audio documents. But recently, ocenaudio on my Macbook is very unstable, the sound it plays is too strange, just like somepeople talk with her/his mounth closed.

I’v tried substitutes to ocenaudio, like Audacity and Ardour, but UI of Audacity is bad designed and Ardour 5 is for people to record and edit, not for me.

I finally fixed the problem on my Ocenaudio. It turned out when I adjest the buffer size, it works fine again.

Go to Preference/Sound/Buffer Size, if it is 1024 samples, then I change it to 512 samples, if it is 512 samples, I change it to 1024 samples.

I could edit my audio document now ヽ( ^∀^)ﾉ
